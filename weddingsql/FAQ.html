Frequently Asked Questions

When is the wedding date?

July 2, 2022

Where is the wedding?

Cranberry Creek Gardens. Click here for more details.

What time do I arrive?

The reception starts 5:30pm and dinner and speeches will begin at 7pm. The reception will last until 12am.

What is the dress code?

Formal. Please also note that women's heals aren't recommeded as the venue is outdoors and there are grassy areas. Please wear flats!

What do I bring for a wedding gift?

We didn't create a gift registry. We would appreciate if you gave us cash gifts only. This will help us cover the costs of the wedding!

The place is far, and I want to sleep over. Is there a place for me to stay?

We blocked off hotels at the Best Western Hotel. There is a free shuttle service to the wedding venue and it will take roughly 15 minutes. You should have indicated within the RSVP form 'Yes' should require us to book the hotel for you. Please note that they are charging $140/night. There is limited space so please submit the RSVP as soon as you can!

Will alcohol be served?

Yes! There is an open bar. If you do not drink, the bar is also serving non-alcoholic drinks as well!
